KBTC core features

The KBTC University is a leading institution in IT education in Myanmar. We offer a wide range of programs and services to meet the needs of our students. It is also a fully-fledged institution with a strong foundation of knowledge and experience in Information Technology. Here is a list of the core features of the university.

Recognized Qualifications

Partnered up with NCC Education in offering qualifications in various areas of specialization.

Teaching Method

Pragmatic approach to unleash greatest potential of all learners.

Facilities

Equipped with learning support materials, respective lab rooms and Office 365 for different fields of specialization.

Consultation Services

Provide comprehensive consultation services to students for further studies.
